Frequently Asked Questions

How do I reset my password?

We use Google OAuth for authentication, so you don't need a separate password. Simply log in with your Google account.

Is my data secure?

Yes! We use industry-standard security measures and only share data with AI providers as needed to process your requests. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

Which AI models do you support?

We currently support GPT-3.5 Turbo, GPT-4, GPT-4 Turbo, and DeepSeek Chat. The system automatically selects the best model based on your prompt.
